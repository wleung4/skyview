SkyView

  • Instructions:
  • Enter an airport name into the search bar
  • Resulting airports matching what was entered will be shown
  • After searching, click on any of the planes numbers to see the
    path of the plane from the departing airport
  • Adjust the zoom of the map using the scroll wheel
  • Hold and drag the map to move on the map
  • Click the search again button on the top left to search again
API provided by The OpenSky Network, https://opensky-network.org
